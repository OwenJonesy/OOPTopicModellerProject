The Premier League is the top tier of professional football leagues in England, comprising 20 teams. The league was established in 1992 and has since become one of the most popular and lucrative football leagues in the world, with a global following of millions of fans. The league is known for its fast-paced and highly competitive style of play, and is home to some of the world's best football clubs, including Manchester United, Liverpool, Chelsea, Arsenal, Manchester City and Tottenham Hotspur. Each season, the teams compete for the title of Premier League champions, as well as qualification for European competitions and avoiding relegation to lower leagues. The Premier League is not just a football league, it has become a cultural phenomenon, with its own unique style and traditions that capture the imagination of fans across the globe.
